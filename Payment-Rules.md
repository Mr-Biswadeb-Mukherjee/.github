# Payment & Support Rules

These rules define how voluntary financial contributions are handled when supporting the research and projects published by **Biswadeb Mukherjee's Lab**.

By making a contribution through any supported platform, you acknowledge and agree to the following terms.

---

## 1. Nature of Support

Payments are considered **voluntary contributions** intended to support independent research and project development.

- Contributions do not establish a **client, vendor, partner, or representative relationship**.
- A contribution does not grant access to private consulting, custom work, or private research material.
- Contributions do not guarantee direct responses, technical assistance, or personalised support.

---

## 2. Scope & Guarantees

Contributions are provided without expectation of service delivery.

- No delivery timelines, development schedules, or support service level agreements (SLAs) are implied.
- Contributions cannot be used to influence research outcomes, publication timing, or technical findings.
- Access to public research, projects, and documentation remains independent of contribution status.

---

## 3. Refunds & Disputes

Contributions are generally **non-refundable**.

- Refunds may only occur where required by applicable law or payment platform policies.
- If a payment issue occurs, contributors should first use the dispute resolution process provided by the payment platform.
- Fraudulent payments, chargeback abuse, or identity misuse may be treated as abuse cases and handled accordingly.

---

## 4. Prohibited Payment Intent

Financial contributions must not be used to request or encourage unlawful or harmful activities.

- Payments must not request illegal activity or assistance in conducting unlawful operations.
- Payments must not request malware distribution, exploit delivery, or operational assistance intended to harm systems or organisations.
- Any coercive, manipulative, or abusive payment behaviour will result in the rejection of the contribution.
